# Numeria Entity System

**Version:** 1.0 — Genesis

---

# Purpose

The Entity System defines the canonical structure for every object that exists within the Numeria universe.

An Entity is anything that can be identified, described, related to another entity, or used in learning.

Everything in Numeria is an Entity.

---

# Philosophy

Mathematics is a network of connected ideas.

Stories are a network of connected characters.

Knowledge is a network of connected concepts.

The Entity System unifies these worlds into a single model.

Instead of separating mathematics, storytelling, curriculum, and technology, Numeria represents them using one shared language.

---

# What Is an Entity?

An Entity is a uniquely identifiable concept within the Numeria universe.

Examples include:

- Derivative
- Delta
- Integral
- Infinity
- The Book of Change
- The Silver Delta
- Numeria Academy
- Kingdom of Functions
- Limits
- Variables
- Coordinate Plane

Each Entity has identity, meaning, relationships, and educational value.

---

# Entity Categories

Entities belong to one primary category.

## Character

A personified mathematical concept.

Examples:

- Derivative
- Integral
- Delta

---

## Mathematical Concept

A formal mathematical idea.

Examples:

- Function
- Limit
- Matrix
- Vector

---

## Symbol

A mathematical notation.

Examples:

- Δ
- ∫
- π
- Σ

---

## Artifact

An important object within the universe.

Examples:

- Book of Change
- Silver Delta
- Compass of Infinity

---

## Location

A place in the Numeria universe.

Examples:

- Numeria Academy
- Kingdom of Functions
- Valley of Limits

---

## Organization

A structured group.

Examples:

- Council of Constants
- Explorers Guild
- Academy of Calculus

---

## Story

A narrative work.

Examples:

- Detective Derivative
- The Fastest Sunflower

# Canonical Entity Fields

Every Entity should define:

- Unique ID
- Name
- Display Name
- Category
- Description
- Educational Purpose
- Mathematical Meaning
- Story Role
- Visual Identity
- Personality
- First Appearance
- Canon Status
- Tags
- Related Entities

These fields form the canonical representation across all applications.

---

# Relationships

Entities become powerful through their relationships.

Examples include:

- teaches
- learns_from
- protects
- opposes
- discovers
- transforms_into
- represents
- explains
- depends_on
- introduces
- created_by
- appears_in

Relationships form the living graph of Numeria.

---

# Visual Identity

Every Entity should have a recognizable visual identity.

This may include:

- Signature colors
- Icon
- Shape language
- Clothing
- Symbols
- Animation style

Learners should recognize important entities instantly.

---

# Educational Role

Every Entity exists to improve learning.

Ask:

"What understanding becomes easier because this Entity exists?"

If an Entity does not improve learning, reconsider its purpose.

---

# Canon

An Entity has only one canonical definition.

Stories may reveal new aspects of an Entity.

They may never contradict its established mathematical meaning.

Mathematical truth always takes precedence.

---

# AI Integration

The Entity System is designed for both humans and artificial intelligence.

AI assistants should understand:

- Entity identity
- Relationships
- Educational purpose
- Story context
- Mathematical meaning
- Canonical history

This enables consistent answers across every Numeria experience.

---

The Entity System is the foundation of the Numeria universe.

Every story begins with Entities.

Every lesson connects Entities.

Every graph visualizes Entities.

Every learner remembers Entities.

Entities are the language through which Numeria brings mathematics to life.
