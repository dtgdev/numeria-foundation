Chapter Purpose Guide

Book: Detective Danny and the Growing Shoes Mystery
Status: Working Draft
Project: Project Storybook

This document defines the storytelling, educational, and emotional purpose of each chapter.

It helps ensure that every chapter moves the mystery forward, strengthens the characters, and introduces mathematics naturally through discovery.

Chapter	Working Title	Story Purpose	Educational Purpose	Emotional Goal
1	A Very Strange Morning	Introduce Danny, Ben, and the mystery of the tight shoes	Model careful observation and avoiding quick conclusions	Curiosity
2	The Shoes That Fit Yesterday	Confirm that Ben’s shoes really have become too tight	Compare past and present measurements	Surprise
3	Mr. Cobbler’s Measurements	Introduce evidence from the village shoemaker	Show why reliable measurements matter	Investigation
4	The Footprints Tell a Story	Danny discovers that Ben’s footprints are changing	Recognize patterns across time	Wonder
5	A Clue in the Forest	Danny and Ben trace the mystery to an unusual plant	Connect cause, effect, and change	Suspense
6	Two Centimeters a Day	Danny calculates how quickly Ben’s feet are growing	Introduce rate of change in intuitive language	Discovery
7	Danny’s Special Word	Danny reveals the mathematical word “derivative”	Connect the story experience to mathematical vocabulary	Understanding
8	The Junior Detective Test	Ben and the reader use Danny’s method to solve a new change mystery	Apply observation, comparison, and rate-of-change thinking	Confidence and celebration

⸻

Chapter Design Rules

Every chapter should:

1. Move the mystery forward.
2. Give the reader at least one new clue.
3. Keep Danny and Ben actively involved.
4. Avoid formal mathematics until the reader has developed intuition.
5. End with curiosity, surprise, or a reason to continue.
6. Let the reader participate in the investigation.
7. Preserve a warm, humorous, and adventurous tone.

⸻

Danny’s Detective Method

The story should repeatedly reinforce Danny’s five-step method:

1. Notice
2. Wonder
3. Investigate
4. Discover
5. Share

Danny should model this process through his actions rather than explain it as a lesson.

⸻

Mathematical Journey

The mathematical progression of the book is:

Something feels different
        ↓
Compare yesterday and today
        ↓
Measure the difference
        ↓
Observe repeated change
        ↓
Describe how fast the change occurs
        ↓
Introduce the word derivative

The reader should understand the idea before encountering the vocabulary.
