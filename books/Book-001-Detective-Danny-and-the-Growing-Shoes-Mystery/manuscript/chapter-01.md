Chapter 1

A Very Strange Morning

The morning sun peeked over the rooftops of Numeria Village.

Golden light danced across the little houses.

Birds sang cheerful songs.

Fresh bread filled the air with its warm, delicious smell.

It looked like an ordinary morning.

But Danny knew something important.

The most interesting mysteries almost always begin on ordinary days.

Danny grabbed his little blue backpack and slung it over his shoulders.

Inside were the things he never left home without.

His detective notebook.

A sharp pencil.

A measuring tape.

A magnifying glass.

And tucked safely into a soft cloth pouch…

His favorite invention.

His Change Glasses.

Danny smiled.

“You never know when the world will leave a clue.”

Just then—

“DANNY!”

A familiar voice echoed down the street.

Danny turned around.

His best friend, Ben, was running toward him.

Well…

Trying to run.

Ben’s arms pumped back and forth.

His face looked determined.

But every few steps he slowed down and made a funny little hop.

Hop.

Step.

Hop.

Step.

Danny watched carefully.

He didn’t laugh.

He simply tilted his head.

“Hmm…”

Ben finally reached him.

He bent over, trying to catch his breath.

“Danny…”

puff…

“I think…”

puff…

“…my shoes are shrinking!”

Danny blinked.

“Shrinking?”

Ben nodded very seriously.

“They fit perfectly yesterday.”

He wiggled his toes.

“Today they feel squished!”

Danny looked down at Ben’s shoes.

Then he looked at Ben.

Then he looked at the ground.

He stayed quiet for a moment.

Finally, he smiled.

Instead of answering, Danny reached into his backpack.

He pulled out his small detective notebook.

On the very first empty page, he carefully wrote:

Case #001

The Growing Shoes Mystery

He closed the notebook with a gentle snap.

“I don’t know if your shoes are shrinking,” Danny said.

“But I do know one thing.”

Ben looked up.

“What’s that?”

Danny grinned.

“I think today’s going to be a very interesting day.”

Together, the two friends walked down the village road, completely unaware that this tiny mystery was about to become Danny’s very first detective case.

And somewhere, hidden in Numeria Village…

the first clue was already waiting.
