Page One

Long before Derivative was born…

the Silver Delta had already become a legend.

No one knew who had made it.

No one knew how old it was.

Some said it was forged from a silver star that had fallen from the sky.

Others believed it had been crafted by the very first mathematicians.

Everyone agreed on one thing.

The necklace never chose the strongest child.

It never chose the fastest.

It never chose the smartest.

The Silver Delta always chose the child who never stopped asking,

“Why?”

And this time…

it chose Derivative.
