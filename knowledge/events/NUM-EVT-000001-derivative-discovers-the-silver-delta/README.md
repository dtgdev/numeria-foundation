# Derivative Discovers the Silver Delta

**Event ID:** NUM-EVT-000001

## Overview

This is the first major historical event in the Numeria canon.

Delta entrusts the Silver Delta to Derivative, recognizing his curiosity,
kindness, and determination to understand the world through careful
observation.

The event begins Derivative's lifelong journey of investigating change.

## Canon Importance

This event establishes:

- the origin of Derivative's mission
- the first appearance of the Silver Delta in active use
- the beginning of Book 1
- the foundation of future adventures

This event is a permanent part of Numeria history.
