# Guardians of Numeria

**Organization ID:** NUM-ORG-000001  
**Status:** CANON

## Mission

The Guardians of Numeria protect mathematical knowledge and help every
learner discover the beauty of mathematics through curiosity and
understanding.

## Members

Current founding members:

- Derivative
- Delta
- Function

Future Guardians will join as new mathematical concepts are introduced.

## Canon Rule

Guardians never compete for power.

Each Guardian protects a different mathematical idea.

Together they preserve the balance of Numeria.
