Origin of the Silver Delta

The true maker of the Silver Delta is unknown.

Some stories claim it was forged from a silver star that fell beyond the Infinity Frontier.

Others say it was created by the earliest mathematical explorers, who wanted future generations to remember that the world is always changing.

The oldest surviving description appears in the Book of Change:

The Silver Delta does not choose the child with every answer.
It chooses the child who continues asking why.

Across generations, the pendant has passed from one Keeper to another.

Each Keeper used it to observe change, uncover hidden patterns, and record discoveries for those who would follow.

Derivative is its current Keeper.

The complete history of the Silver Delta has not yet been revealed.
