Rules of the Silver Delta

These rules are immutable unless superseded through an approved canonical decision.

Rule 1 — Only One Exists

There is only one Silver Delta in Numeria.

Copies, replicas, illustrations, or symbolic representations may exist, but only one pendant possesses its canonical ability.

Rule 2 — It Chooses Its Keeper

The Silver Delta cannot be claimed through strength, wealth, authority, inheritance alone, or force.

It chooses a Keeper who demonstrates curiosity, patience, honesty, kindness, and respect for evidence.

Rule 3 — It Reveals Patterns

The Silver Delta reveals relationships among observed changes.

It may highlight repetition, direction, sequence, difference, or consistency.

Rule 4 — It Never Gives Answers

The Silver Delta does not explain what caused a pattern.

It does not determine what the characters should do.

The Keeper and companions must interpret the evidence.

Rule 5 — It Never Predicts With Certainty

The pendant may reveal that a pattern is continuing.

It may help characters form a reasonable expectation.

It must never guarantee the future.

Rule 6 — It Does Not Create Change

The Silver Delta detects and reveals change already occurring in the world.

It does not cause growth, motion, shrinking, melting, acceleration, or transformation.

Rule 7 — It Cannot Replace Measurement

The Keeper must observe, compare, measure, record, or gather evidence before a complete pattern can be revealed.

Rule 8 — It Cannot Be Commanded

Derivative cannot activate the Silver Delta merely because he wants an answer.

The pendant responds only when a meaningful pattern of change is present.

Rule 9 — It Becomes Quiet When Change Stops

When the relevant change has stopped, the Silver Delta becomes cool and still.

Rule 10 — It Serves Understanding

The Silver Delta exists to deepen curiosity and understanding.

It must never be used to frighten, dominate, embarrass, or control others.

Immutable Story Rule

Magic may reveal the pattern. Thinking must solve the mystery.
