# Book of Change

**Artifact ID:** NUM-ART-000002  
**Status:** CANON  
**Keeper:** Derivative  
**Primary Location:** Observatory of Change

> The Book records no guess as truth and reveals no answer before it is understood.

## Overview

The Book of Change is one of Numeria's oldest artifacts.

It preserves discoveries about how the world moves, grows, slows,
accelerates, transforms, and responds over time.

Its pages appear empty to anyone seeking an effortless answer.

When a discovery is supported by observation, evidence, and reasoning,
the Book records it permanently.

## Story Purpose

The Book of Change:

- preserves discoveries from Derivative's investigations
- connects current mysteries to earlier patterns
- shows how understanding develops over time
- rewards evidence rather than guessing
- introduces children to recording and revising knowledge

## Canon Rules

The Book of Change never solves a mystery.

It may reveal an earlier observation or related pattern, but Derivative
must interpret the evidence himself.

The Book cannot record something as true unless it has been properly
understood.

Its contents may grow, but established mathematical truth cannot be
rewritten incorrectly.

## Educational Meaning

The Book represents:

- scientific records
- mathematical notebooks
- accumulated knowledge
- evidence-based reasoning
- learning across generations

It teaches that discoveries become valuable when they are recorded,
tested, explained, and shared.
