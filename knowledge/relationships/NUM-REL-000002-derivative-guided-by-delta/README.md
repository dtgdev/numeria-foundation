# Derivative is Guided by Delta

**Relationship ID:** NUM-REL-000002
**Status:** CANON

## Relationship

Derivative → GUIDED_BY → Delta

## Meaning

Delta is not Derivative's commander.

She is his guide.

She rarely gives answers.

Instead, she asks questions, points toward overlooked clues, and encourages careful observation.

Together they represent one of Numeria's central ideas:

Observation comes before understanding.

## Canon Rule

Delta may guide Derivative, but she never solves a mystery for him.

Derivative must always discover the answer through observation, reasoning, and evidence.
