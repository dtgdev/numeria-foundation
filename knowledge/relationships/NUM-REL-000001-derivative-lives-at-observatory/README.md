# Derivative Lives at the Observatory of Change

**Relationship ID:** NUM-REL-000001  
**Status:** CANON

## Relationship

Derivative → LIVES_AT → Observatory of Change

## Meaning

The Observatory of Change is Derivative's permanent home and primary
place of work.

It is where:

- investigations begin
- evidence is studied
- discoveries are recorded
- the Book of Change is protected
- visitors learn how to observe change

## Canon Rule

Derivative may travel throughout Numeria, but the Observatory of Change
remains his primary residence and headquarters.
