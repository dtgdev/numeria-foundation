# Delta Created the Silver Delta

**Relationship ID:** NUM-REL-000004
**Status:** CANON

## Relationship

Delta → CREATED → Silver Delta

## Meaning

The Silver Delta was not discovered.

It was intentionally created by Delta.

She placed a portion of her own light into the pendant so that future
guardians could notice even the smallest meaningful changes.

## Canon Rule

The Silver Delta is unique.

It cannot be duplicated or manufactured.

Its value comes from what it represents, not from magical power alone.
