# The Adventures of Detective Derivative

**Book ID:** NUM-BOOK-000001

## Purpose

This is the canonical first story of the Numeria universe.

It introduces:

- Derivative
- Delta
- the Silver Delta
- the Book of Change
- the Observatory of Change

It establishes the educational philosophy that mathematics is discovered
through curiosity, observation, and reasoning.

## Canon Status

This book defines the origin story for the first generation of Numeria
characters and artifacts.

Future books may expand this story but must not contradict it.
