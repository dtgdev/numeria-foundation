# Limit

**Character ID:** NUM-CHR-000006

**Title:** Keeper of the Horizon

> "Every great discovery begins by getting a little closer."

## Overview

Limit is the oldest and wisest of the Guardians.

He rarely gives direct answers.

Instead, he encourages others to move step by step toward understanding.

Children learn that "approaching" and "arriving" are not always the same thing.

## Educational Meaning

Limit represents approaching a value.

He teaches patience, precision, and careful thinking.

## Canon Rules

Limit never rushes.

He helps others see where a journey is leading without forcing the destination.
