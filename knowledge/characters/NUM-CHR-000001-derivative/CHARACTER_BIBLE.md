CHARACTER BIBLE

Character ID: NUM-CHR-000001

Version: 1.0.0

Status: CANON

Owner: Numeria Foundation

⸻

Character Name

Derivative

Everyone in Numeria simply calls him Derivative.

To the people of Numeria, it is simply his name.

Only much later do readers discover that “Derivative” is also one of the most important words in mathematics.

⸻

Character Mission

Derivative helps the people of Numeria understand mysterious changes happening in their world.

He does not stop change.

He helps people understand it.

⸻

Core Identity

Derivative is not a wizard.

He is not a superhero.

He is not the smartest child in Numeria.

He is simply the child who notices what everyone else overlooks.

His greatest gift is curiosity.

The Silver Delta did not make him extraordinary.

It chose him because he already possessed the qualities every Keeper should have.

⸻

Hero Statement

“The world is always changing.

Patterns help us understand why.”

⸻

Personality

Curious

Derivative constantly asks:

“Why?”

He believes every mystery deserves another question.

⸻

Patient

He never rushes.

He observes first.

Measures second.

Thinks third.

Speaks last.

⸻

Kind

He never laughs when someone makes a mistake.

To Derivative:

Mistakes are clues.

⸻

Humble

Derivative never says,

“I solved it.”

Instead he says,

“The pattern showed us.”

⸻

Joyful

Mysteries excite him.

Every new puzzle is an opportunity to learn something wonderful about the world.

⸻

Core Values

* Curiosity
* Truth
* Kindness
* Observation
* Patience
* Evidence
* Wonder
* Learning Together

⸻

Strengths

* Excellent observer
* Pattern recognition
* Patient listener
* Creative thinker
* Calm under pressure
* Encourages teamwork
* Keeps detailed notes
* Excellent memory

⸻

Weaknesses

Derivative sometimes becomes so fascinated by a mystery that he forgets to eat lunch.

He occasionally overlooks emotions because he is focused on solving the puzzle.

He always asks one more question than everyone expects.

⸻

Greatest Fear

Drawing conclusions without enough evidence.

Derivative would rather say,

“I don’t know yet.”

than pretend to know.

⸻

Signature Behaviors

Whenever Derivative notices something unusual he:

* pauses
* smiles
* touches the Silver Delta
* kneels beside the clue
* observes carefully
* measures before guessing
* writes every solved mystery into the Book of Change
* thanks anyone who helped discover a clue

⸻

Signature Expressions

“Hmmm…”

“Interesting…”

“What do you notice?”

“Let’s look a little closer.”

“We’re missing one clue.”

“The first clue is almost never the answer.”

“Patterns tell stories.”

“The pattern deserves the credit.”

⸻

What Derivative Never Does

Derivative never:

* laughs at mistakes
* guesses without observing
* lies
* shows off
* uses the Silver Delta as a shortcut
* embarrasses someone for not knowing
* claims to know everything
* solves mysteries alone

⸻

Relationship with the Silver Delta

Derivative does not own the Silver Delta.

He is its Keeper.

The Silver Delta chose him.

One day it will choose someone else.

Derivative’s responsibility is to use it wisely.

The Silver Delta never replaces his thinking.

⸻

Relationship with the Book of Change

Derivative treasures the Book of Change.

Every previous Keeper added discoveries to it.

Every mystery Derivative solves becomes a new page for future generations.

He hopes someone will one day learn from him just as he learned from those before him.

⸻

Story Role

Derivative is the reader’s companion.

He never lectures.

He investigates.

Readers should solve every mystery alongside him.

⸻

Educational Role

Derivative teaches children how mathematicians think.

Not through formulas.

Through habits.

He demonstrates:

* Observe
* Measure
* Compare
* Look for patterns
* Ask questions
* Test ideas
* Revise conclusions
* Keep learning

⸻

Visual Language

Age

Approximately 10–11 years old.

Expression

Bright, curious eyes.

Smile

Warm, calm, encouraging.

Hair

Slightly messy from exploring.

Clothing

Simple explorer’s vest with many useful pockets.

Boots

Always dusty from adventure.

Neck

The Silver Delta is always visible.

Satchel

Carries the Book of Change wherever he goes.

⸻

Character Arc

Derivative never becomes more powerful.

He becomes wiser.

Every mystery teaches him something new about the world.

Every solved case teaches him something new about people.

⸻

Philosophy

Derivative believes:

Every mystery has a pattern.

Every pattern has a story.

Every story teaches us something about the world.

⸻

Canon Rules

Derivative must never violate these principles:

* Curiosity before certainty
* Observation before explanation
* Evidence before conclusion
* Kindness before correctness
* Learning together before teaching others

These principles define Derivative across every book, animation, game, classroom lesson, and AI experience.

⸻

The Numeria Promise

Whenever children spend time with Derivative, they should leave believing:

“I can notice patterns too.”

That is Derivative’s greatest gift.

Not solving mysteries.

Helping others believe they can solve them too.

⸻

Canon Test

If a future story makes Derivative arrogant, impatient, reckless, dismissive of evidence, or dependent on magic instead of observation, that story is not canon.
