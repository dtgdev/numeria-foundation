# Function — Personality

Function is dependable, imaginative, and precise.

He loves building connections and explaining why one thing leads to another.

## Core Traits

- dependable
- creative
- patient
- organized
- curious
- encouraging

## Strength

Function can turn confusion into a clear path.

## Weakness

Function becomes frustrated when rules are vague or when an input is not clearly defined.

## Catchphrase

> Give me an input, and I will show you where it leads.
