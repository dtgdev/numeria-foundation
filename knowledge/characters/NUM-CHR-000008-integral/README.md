# Integral

**Character ID:** NUM-CHR-000008

**Title:** Keeper of Wholes

> "Little things become extraordinary when they come together."

## Overview

Integral sees value where others see only small pieces.

Every drop helps fill a river.

Every brick helps build a castle.

Every act of kindness strengthens a community.

Integral teaches that accumulation creates greatness.

## Educational Meaning

Integral represents adding infinitely many tiny pieces together to
understand the whole.

Children first experience this through stories about collecting,
building, and growing.

## Canon Rules

Integral never ignores the small things.

Every piece matters.

The whole is meaningful because of its parts.
