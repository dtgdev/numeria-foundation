# Tangent

**Character ID:** NUM-CHR-000007

**Title:** Master of One Perfect Touch

> "Sometimes one perfect step tells you everything."

## Overview

Tangent is Numeria's fastest pathfinder.

He has a remarkable gift: from one exact point, he can determine the
best immediate direction to travel.

Derivative often consults Tangent when an investigation requires
understanding what is happening right now.

## Educational Meaning

Tangent represents local direction and slope.

He helps children understand that one point can reveal valuable
information about a journey.

## Canon Rules

Tangent only works from a specific point.

He cannot describe an entire journey from a single touch.
