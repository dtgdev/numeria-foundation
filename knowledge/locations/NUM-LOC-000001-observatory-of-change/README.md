# Observatory of Change

**Entity ID:** NUM-LOC-000001  
**Status:** CANON  
**Primary Resident:** Derivative

> From the highest hill in Numeria, every change leaves a clue.

## Overview

The Observatory of Change is Derivative's home, laboratory, library,
and investigation center.

It stands on the highest hill in Numeria, giving Derivative a clear view
of rivers, forests, mountains, roads, gardens, clocks, and stars.

The Observatory is designed to help its residents answer one question:

> What is changing, and how?

## Story Purpose

The Observatory is the place where:

- mysteries are first detected
- clues are compared
- maps and patterns are studied
- the Silver Delta begins to glow
- the Book of Change records new discoveries
- Derivative prepares for an investigation

## Educational Purpose

The location introduces children to the idea that understanding change
begins with careful observation.

The Observatory should visually reinforce:

- motion over time
- growth and decline
- slope and direction
- comparison
- measurement
- patterns
- evidence

## Canon Rules

The Observatory must never solve a mystery automatically.

It provides tools, views, records, and clues, but Derivative must still:

1. ask a question
2. gather evidence
3. compare changes
4. recognize the pattern
5. explain the discovery

## Visual Identity

The Observatory combines:

- a grand treehouse
- a scientific observatory
- a detective workshop
- a magical library

Its main visual elements include:

- silver telescopes
- glowing graphs
- moving maps
- clocks
- measuring instruments
- the Silver Delta symbol
- windows facing every region of Numeria
