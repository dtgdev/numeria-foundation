# Numeria Knowledge Architecture

## Core Principle

Numeria is not a collection of books.

Numeria is a knowledge graph centered on mathematical understanding.

Stories, characters, animations, games, AI tutors, lessons, assessments, and curriculum are all different representations of mathematical knowledge.

## Hierarchy

Knowledge
    ├── Mathematical Concepts
    ├── Characters
    ├── Stories
    ├── Learning Resources
    ├── Assessments
    ├── Historical Context
    ├── Symbols
    ├── Formulae
    └── Relationships

## Everything is Connected

Every entity is connected through explicit relationships.

Examples:

Derivative
    represents
        Rate of Change

Story
    teaches
        Derivative

Animation
    explains
        Derivative

Lesson
    practices
        Derivative

Game
    reinforces
        Derivative

Assessment
    evaluates
        Derivative

## Design Philosophy

Knowledge comes first.

Media comes second.

Technology comes third.

The architecture should allow future learning experiences that do not yet exist.
